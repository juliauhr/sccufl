About SCCUFL-VR


The Special Collections of CU Fairytale Library (SCCUFL) is a virtual reality library built using A-Frame and featuring illustrated and historical fairy tales from the University of Colorado Boulder Libraries' Department of Special Collections, Archives and Preservation.

It can be viewed online at https://juliauhr.github.io/sccufl and is best viewed on an Oculus Go headset.



This project was developed by Julia Uhr with support from the University of Colorado Provost's Fellowship in Information Science. It is currently under development.

For more information or to send us your digitized public domain fairy tale books for inclusion in SCCUFL-VR, please email
